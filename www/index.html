Welcome to Project Play - Squamish

Welcome to Project Play Family Centre, a family owned and operated home preschool where the focus is on learning through play.

We welcome children from 2.5 to 5 years of age, in our multi-age preschool classroom. At Project Play, children will be supported to develop their imagination and creativity, their knowledge, independent thinking skills and sense of wonder through exploring their own ideas and interests. This learning is facilitated in an environment that is friendly, homely, secure and inviting, rich with experiences, adventures and by a team of warm, nurturing, professional educators.

We share the vision, principles and learning goals provided by the British Columbia Early Learning Framework.

"All children are born with a curiosity about themselves, other people, and the world around them, and in this sense, are born learners" (BC Early Learning Framework)

Project Play provides families with a premium quality preschool program located close to Brennan Centre and only a short drive from downtown Squamish.
